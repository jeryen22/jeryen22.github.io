Summary of the book.
Percy, Annabeth, Thalia and Grover go to Western Hall to recruit two new demigods: Nico di Angelo and Bianca di Angelo, The four pretend to party at the dance and kept a eye at the two. Percy sees that Dr. Thorn the monster or Vice principal is gone with the two kids. Percy decides to go find Dr. Thorn, but gets captured by him. Dr. Thorn talks about the General’s evil plan called the Great Stirring, which is the time where they are reviving the worst monsters of history. Annabeth, Thalia and Grover then come to rescue Percy and fights Dr. Thorn. The hunters of Artemis appear and take down Dr. Thorn, but takes Annabeth down with him. They return to camp and Bianca di Angelo joins the hunters of Artemis. The Oracle says a prophecy that was: Five shall go west to the Goddess in chains, One shall be lost in the land without rain, The bane of Olympus shows the trail, Campers and Hunters combined prevail, The Titan’s curse must one withstand, And one shall perish by a parents hand. Chiron the camp leader, says that three hunters and two campers. Zoe nightshade, Bianca and Phoebe as the hunters go to the quest. Grover goes but Thalia gets the fifth part of it. During that night Phoebe get pranked into wearing a poisoned shirt making the hunters believe that it was part of the quest. At night Percy finds his friend Blackjack, who was a pegasus, who says that the hippocampi are asking for help. Percy goes down and finds a cow trapped under a net. Percy brings out his sword to cut it but the cow gets scared and thrashes around. Percy untangles the net and goes back up to blackjack and flies back to his cabin. On the way, Percy sees Nico who was spying on the hunters, who were talking about Phoebe. Percy used Annabeth’s invisibility cap and snuck up on Nico. When the hunters went to the big house to share the news Percy took off the hat and talked to Nico. Nico says that he wants to protect his sister Bianca, and decides that Percy is going to the quest and he could help protect Bianca. Percy promises that he will and goes on the quest.
